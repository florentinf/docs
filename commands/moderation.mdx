title: "Moderation"
---

Neon has many moderation commands to help keep your server safe and organized.

## What you can do

- Ban, kick, or warn users
- Jail or timeout troublemakers
- Lock or unlock channels
- Bulk delete messages
- Manage roles and nicknames
- Check user and mod histories

Most commands require moderation permissions.

See the [full command list]() for details and usage examples.
